Top three activities to do in Singapore

Marvel at Marina Bay Sands & Gardens by the Bay

Experience Singapore’s iconic skyline at Marina Bay Sands and stroll through the futuristic Supertree Grove at Gardens by the Bay. Don’t miss the evening light and sound show!

Savour Local Delights at a Hawker Centre

Indulge in authentic Singaporean cuisine—try Hainanese chicken rice, laksa, and chilli crab at the city’s vibrant hawker centres for a true taste of local life.

Relax and Play at Sentosa Island

Enjoy sun, sand, and adventure at Sentosa. From Universal Studios Singapore to tranquil beaches and the S.E.A. Aquarium, there’s fun for everyone.
